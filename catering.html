How It Works

  1. Initial Consultation

    Contact us to discuss your event details, including date, time, number of guests, and dietary requirements.

  2. Menu Selection

    Choose from our catering menus or work with us to create a custom menu for your event.

  3. Quote & Confirmation

    We'll provide a detailed quote and proposal. A 50% deposit is required to secure your date.

  4. Final Details

    Two weeks before your event, we'll confirm all details and make any necessary adjustments.

  5. Event Day

    We'll arrive early to set up and ensure everything is perfect for your event.
