Our Story

From a small home kitchen to Sweetville's favorite bakery,
we've been crafting delicious memories since 2015.

The Sweet Beginning

What started as a passion project in Cody's home kitchen has blossomed into Sweetville's most beloved bakery. Our journey began in 2015 when Cody, a self-taught baker with a dream, started selling homemade cookies and cakes at local farmers' markets. The overwhelming positive response from the community inspired us to open our first storefront in 2017.

2015

The Beginning

Our Values

At Cody's Sweet Treats, we believe in more than just making delicious baked goods. Our core values guide everything we do, from ingredient selection to customer service.

Quality Ingredients

Made with Love

What Makes Us Special
